Troubleshooting

  • All received photos and videos you can find in Camera Roll (Saved photos) album and in virtual album "VideoTransfer in native Photos app on your device. If you sent pics/videos on your computer you can find it in your default download folder as zip archive with name like "Photos_current_date.zip"
  • Supported files format. You can transfer only files which supported by native Photo app on your device.
    Pictures: .tiff, .tif; .jpg, .jpeg; .gif; .png; .bmp, .BMPf; .ico; .cur; .
    Videos: .mov, .mp4, .mpv, and .3gp and using one of the following compression standards: H.264 Baseline Profile Level 3.0 video, up to 640 x 480 at 30 fps.
    MPEG-4 Part 2 video (Simple Profile)
  • Double check that your device and computer are connected to the same Wi-Fi network.
  • The screen of both devices must be active and Image Transfer must be running when you attempt to connect.
  • While connecting with computer, make sure that you type the address directly in the address bar and not in the search bar, use it exactly as it appears in the Image Transfer including "http://" part.
  • Remember, if you are using corporate, hotel or public network, it can be configured to prevent connections between devices. If it is so, try to connect both devices to another Wi-Fi network.
  • For home network, try to reboot your Wi-Fi router and connect to the network again.
  • Skype call, on-line radio or other applications which streaming data to your computer, may interrupt the connection between devices, try to quit.
